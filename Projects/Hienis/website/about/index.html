We also provide turnkey thermal & acoustic insulation contracting services.

We, Hi-Tech Energy Insulations, based in Chennai, were established in March 2012 with a vision to meet the growing needs of HVAC, thermal, and acoustic insulation solutions across various industries.

Over the years, we have evolved into a complete turnkey contractor for thermal and acoustic insulation projects, offering end-to-end solutions from supply to execution. We are also engaged in the supply of PEB (Pre-Engineered Building) materials, catering to both the industrial and building sectors.

Our client base includes companies in process industries, manufacturing units, and HVAC system integrators, with whom we maintain strong and reliable working relationships. We have consistently delivered quality products and services, tailored to the specific requirements of each project.

We understand the critical need for effective insulation in enhancing energy efficiency and operational performance. With this in mind, we offer a comprehensive range of products, services, and customized solutions, ensuring that every business objective is met with precision and professionalism.
