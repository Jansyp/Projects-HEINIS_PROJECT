Product Overview

Smoke exhaust units are designed to remove smoke and provide emergency ventilation in case of fire or hazardous events. They are essential for safety in large buildings and industrial facilities.

Applications

  • Smoke extraction in commercial and industrial buildings
  • Emergency ventilation systems
  • Fire safety installations
