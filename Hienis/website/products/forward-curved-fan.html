Product Overview

The Forward Curved Centrifugal Fan is engineered for high airflow at low to medium pressures, making it ideal for HVAC systems, air handling units, and general ventilation. Its forward-curved blades ensure quiet operation and efficient air delivery.

Key Features

  • High airflow with low noise
  • Forward-curved impeller for efficient air movement
  • Robust steel or aluminum construction
  • Customizable sizes and mounting options
  • Corrosion-resistant coatings available
  • Energy-efficient motor options

Applications

  • HVAC air handling units
  • Industrial ventilation
  • Cleanrooms and laboratories
  • Commercial kitchens and exhaust systems
  • Cooling of electrical panels and machinery

Technical Specifications

  • Airflow: 500 – 20,000 m³/h (customizable)
  • Pressure: up to 800 Pa
  • Voltage: 230V/415V, 50Hz (others on request)
  • Material: Galvanized steel, aluminum, or stainless steel
  • Mounting: Floor, wall, or duct
